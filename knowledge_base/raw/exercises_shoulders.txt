EXERCISE: Overhead Press
Muscle Group: Shoulders
Type: Compound
Primary Muscles: Anterior deltoid, Lateral deltoid
Secondary Muscles: Triceps, Upper trapezius, Core
Equipment: Barbell

Execution:
Bar at shoulder height in rack. Press overhead to full lockout. Bar travels in slight arc around face. Brace core throughout. Dumbbell variation: more range of motion, greater stability demand.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 4 sets × 3–5 reps | Rest: 180s | Tempo: 2-0-X-0
  • Hypertrophy: 4 sets × 8–12 reps | Rest: 90s | Tempo: 3-0-1-0
  • Fat_loss: 3 sets × 10–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Endurance: 3 sets × 15–20 reps | Rest: 45s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • General: 3 sets × 10–12 reps | Rest: 75s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0

Progressive overload:
  • Beginner: +1.25kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 6 weeks
  • Intermediate: +1.25kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 4 weeks
  • Advanced: +0.5kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 4 weeks

EXERCISE: Lateral Raise
Muscle Group: Shoulders
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Lateral deltoid
Secondary Muscles: Supraspinatus
Equipment: Dumbbell

Execution:
Stand with dumbbells at sides. Raise arms to shoulder height in arc. Slight forward lean and thumb-down position to bias lateral head. Control descent — 3 seconds down.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 3 sets × 5–8 reps | Rest: 120s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Hypertrophy: 3 sets × 10–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Fat_loss: 3 sets × 12–15 reps | Rest: 45s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Endurance: 2 sets × 15–20 reps | Rest: 30s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0

EXERCISE: Arnold Press
Muscle Group: Shoulders
Type: Compound
Primary Muscles: Anterior deltoid, Lateral deltoid, Posterior deltoid
Secondary Muscles: Triceps, Upper trapezius
Equipment: Dumbbell

Execution:
Start with palms facing you at shoulder height. Rotate palms outward as you press overhead. Reverse on descent. Full range of motion hits all three deltoid heads.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 4 sets × 3–5 reps | Rest: 180s | Tempo: 2-0-X-0

EXERCISE: Rear Delt Fly
Muscle Group: Shoulders
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Posterior deltoid, Rhomboids
Secondary Muscles: Middle trapezius
Equipment: Dumbbell

Execution:
Hinge forward 45-90°. Raise dumbbells in wide arc to shoulder height. Pinch shoulder blades. Critical for posture and shoulder balance.

Prescription by goal:
