EXERCISE: Barbell Squat
Muscle Group: Legs
Type: Compound
Primary Muscles: Quadriceps, Gluteus maximus, Hamstrings
Secondary Muscles: Adductors, Core, Erector spinae
Equipment: Barbell

Execution:
Bar on upper traps (high bar) or rear delts (low bar). Feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out. Break at hips and knees simultaneously. Descend until hips below knees. Drive through full foot on ascent. Keep chest up, knees tracking toes.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 4 sets × 3–5 reps | Rest: 180s | Tempo: 2-0-X-0
  • Hypertrophy: 4 sets × 8–12 reps | Rest: 90s | Tempo: 3-0-1-0
  • Fat_loss: 3 sets × 10–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Endurance: 3 sets × 15–20 reps | Rest: 45s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • General: 3 sets × 10–12 reps | Rest: 75s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0

Progressive overload:
  • Beginner: +2.5kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 6 weeks
  • Intermediate: +2.5kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 4 weeks
  • Advanced: +1.25kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 4 weeks

EXERCISE: Romanian Deadlift (RDL)
Muscle Group: Legs
Type: Compound
Primary Muscles: Hamstrings, Gluteus maximus
Secondary Muscles: Erector spinae, Adductors
Equipment: Barbell

Execution:
Start standing with barbell. Hinge at hips, pushing them back. Lower bar along legs until strong stretch in hamstrings (~shin level). Drive hips forward to return. Knees slightly bent throughout.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 4 sets × 3–5 reps | Rest: 180s | Tempo: 2-0-X-0
  • Hypertrophy: 4 sets × 8–12 reps | Rest: 90s | Tempo: 3-0-1-0

EXERCISE: Leg Press
Muscle Group: Legs
Type: Compound
Primary Muscles: Quadriceps, Gluteus maximus
Secondary Muscles: Hamstrings, Adductors
Equipment: Machine

Execution:
Feet shoulder-width on platform. Lower until 90° knee angle. Press to near-lockout (don't lock knees). High foot placement = more glutes/hamstrings. Low foot placement = more quads.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 4 sets × 3–5 reps | Rest: 180s | Tempo: 2-0-X-0

EXERCISE: Bulgarian Split Squat
Muscle Group: Legs
Type: Compound
Primary Muscles: Quadriceps, Gluteus maximus
Secondary Muscles: Hamstrings, Adductors, Core
Equipment: Dumbbell

Execution:
Rear foot elevated on bench. Front foot forward enough to maintain vertical shin. Lower rear knee toward floor. Loaded with dumbbells or barbell. Excellent unilateral leg developer.

Prescription by goal:

EXERCISE: Glute Bridge / Hip Thrust
Muscle Group: Legs
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Gluteus maximus, Gluteus medius
Secondary Muscles: Hamstrings, Core
Equipment: Barbell

Execution:
Back on bench, barbell across hips. Drive hips up to full extension. Squeeze glutes at top. Hold 1 second. Best exercise for glute isolation.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 3 sets × 5–8 reps | Rest: 120s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Hypertrophy: 3 sets × 10–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Fat_loss: 3 sets × 12–15 reps | Rest: 45s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0

EXERCISE: Leg Curl
Muscle Group: Legs
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Hamstrings (biceps femoris)
Secondary Muscles: Gastrocnemius
Equipment: Machine

Execution:

EXERCISE: Calf Raise
Muscle Group: Legs
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Gastrocnemius, Soleus
Equipment: Machine

Execution:
Full range of motion — deep stretch at bottom, full plantar flexion at top. 3-second hold at top. Seat calf raises target soleus; standing targets gastrocnemius.
