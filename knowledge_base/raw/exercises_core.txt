EXERCISE: Plank
Muscle Group: Core
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Transverse abdominis, Rectus abdominis
Secondary Muscles: Glutes, Shoulder stabilizers
Equipment: Bodyweight

Execution:
Forearms on floor. Body straight from head to heels. Brace like expecting a punch. Breathe normally. Do NOT hold breath. Progress: weighted vest, feet elevated, single-arm.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 3 sets × 5–8 reps | Rest: 120s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Hypertrophy: 3 sets × 10–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Fat_loss: 3 sets × 12–15 reps | Rest: 45s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Endurance: 2 sets × 15–20 reps | Rest: 30s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • General: 3 sets × 12–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0

Progressive overload:
  • Beginner: +1.25kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 6 weeks
  • Intermediate: +1.25kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 4 weeks
  • Advanced: +0.5kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 4 weeks

EXERCISE: Dead Bug
Muscle Group: Core
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Transverse abdominis, Rectus abdominis
Secondary Muscles: Hip flexors, Shoulder stabilizers
Equipment: Bodyweight

Execution:
Lie on back. Arms straight up, hips/knees at 90°. Lower opposite arm and leg simultaneously while pressing lower back into floor. Return. Alternate sides. Best anti-extension core exercise.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 3 sets × 5–8 reps | Rest: 120s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Hypertrophy: 3 sets × 10–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Fat_loss: 3 sets × 12–15 reps | Rest: 45s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0

EXERCISE: Cable Crunch
Muscle Group: Core
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Rectus abdominis

EXERCISE: Ab Wheel Rollout
Muscle Group: Core
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Rectus abdominis, Transverse abdominis
Secondary Muscles: Latissimus dorsi, Shoulders
Equipment: Bodyweight

Execution:
Kneeling on floor. Roll wheel forward until body nearly parallel to ground. Pull back using abs. Most challenging anti-extension ab exercise.

EXERCISE: McGill Curl-up
Muscle Group: Core
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Rectus abdominis
Secondary Muscles: Neck flexors
Equipment: Bodyweight

Execution:
Lie flat. One knee bent. Place hands under lower back. Lift only head and shoulders, maintaining neutral spine. NOT a full crunch — preserves lumbar spine.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 3 sets × 5–8 reps | Rest: 120s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
