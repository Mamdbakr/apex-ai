EXERCISE: Barbell Curl
Muscle Group: Arms
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Biceps brachii, Brachialis
Secondary Muscles: Forearm flexors
Equipment: Barbell

Execution:
Stand with barbell, shoulder-width underhand grip. Curl to shoulder height, squeeze bicep. Lower in 3 seconds. Keep elbows fixed at sides — no swinging.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 3 sets × 5–8 reps | Rest: 120s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Hypertrophy: 3 sets × 10–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Fat_loss: 3 sets × 12–15 reps | Rest: 45s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Endurance: 2 sets × 15–20 reps | Rest: 30s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • General: 3 sets × 12–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0

Progressive overload:
  • Beginner: +1.25kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 6 weeks
  • Intermediate: +1.25kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 4 weeks
  • Advanced: +0.5kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 4 weeks

EXERCISE: Tricep Pushdown
Muscle Group: Arms
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Triceps brachii (lateral + medial head)
Secondary Muscles: Anconeus
Equipment: Cables

Execution:
Set cable high. Push bar/rope down to full extension. Elbows stay at sides. Squeeze triceps at lockout.

Prescription by goal:

EXERCISE: Skull Crusher
Muscle Group: Arms
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Triceps brachii (long head)
Secondary Muscles: Anconeus
Equipment: Barbell

Execution:

EXERCISE: Preacher Curl
Muscle Group: Arms
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Biceps brachii (short head)
Secondary Muscles: Brachialis
