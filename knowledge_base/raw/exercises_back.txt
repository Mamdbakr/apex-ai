EXERCISE: Deadlift
Muscle Group: Back
Type: Compound
Primary Muscles: Erector spinae, Gluteus maximus, Hamstrings
Secondary Muscles: Latissimus dorsi, Trapezius, Forearms
Equipment: Barbell

Execution:
Bar over mid-foot. Hinge at hips, grip just outside legs. Brace core, pull bar up by driving floor away. Lock hips and knees together at top. Keep bar close to body throughout. Neutral spine mandatory.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 4 sets × 3–5 reps | Rest: 180s | Tempo: 2-0-X-0
  • Hypertrophy: 4 sets × 8–12 reps | Rest: 90s | Tempo: 3-0-1-0
  • Fat_loss: 3 sets × 10–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Endurance: 3 sets × 15–20 reps | Rest: 45s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • General: 3 sets × 10–12 reps | Rest: 75s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0

Progressive overload:
  • Beginner: +1.25kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 6 weeks
  • Intermediate: +1.25kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 4 weeks
  • Advanced: +0.5kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 4 weeks

EXERCISE: Barbell Row
Muscle Group: Back
Type: Compound
Primary Muscles: Latissimus dorsi, Rhomboids, Middle trapezius
Secondary Muscles: Biceps brachii, Rear deltoid
Equipment: Barbell

Execution:
Hinge to ~45°. Pull bar to lower ribcage, leading with elbows. Squeeze shoulder blades at top. Control descent.

EXERCISE: Pull-up / Chin-up
Muscle Group: Back
Type: Compound
Primary Muscles: Latissimus dorsi, Biceps brachii
Secondary Muscles: Rear deltoid, Rhomboids, Core
Equipment: Pull-up Bar

Execution:
Dead hang from bar. Pull until chin clears bar. Pull-up = overhand (more lats). Chin-up = underhand (more biceps). Progress: weighted belt. Regress: band-assisted or lat pulldown.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 4 sets × 3–5 reps | Rest: 180s | Tempo: 2-0-X-0

Muscle Group: Back
Type: Compound
Primary Muscles: Latissimus dorsi, Rhomboids

EXERCISE: Face Pull
Muscle Group: Back
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Rear deltoid, Rotator cuff, Middle trapezius
Secondary Muscles: Rhomboids
Equipment: Cables

Execution:
Set cable at head height. Pull rope to face, separating hands at end. External rotation at end range. Essential for shoulder health.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 3 sets × 5–8 reps | Rest: 120s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Hypertrophy: 3 sets × 10–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Fat_loss: 3 sets × 12–15 reps | Rest: 45s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Endurance: 2 sets × 15–20 reps | Rest: 30s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • General: 3 sets × 12–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
