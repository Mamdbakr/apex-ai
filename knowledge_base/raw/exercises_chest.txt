EXERCISE: Barbell Bench Press
Muscle Group: Chest
Type: Compound
Primary Muscles: Pectoralis major
Secondary Muscles: Anterior deltoid, Triceps brachii
Equipment: Barbell

Execution:
Lie flat on bench. Grip bar slightly wider than shoulder-width. Lower bar to mid-chest with elbows at ~45°. Press back up to full extension. Keep feet flat, slight arch in lower back, shoulder blades retracted.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 4 sets × 3–5 reps | Rest: 180s | Tempo: 2-0-X-0
  • Hypertrophy: 4 sets × 8–12 reps | Rest: 90s | Tempo: 3-0-1-0
  • Fat_loss: 3 sets × 10–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Endurance: 3 sets × 15–20 reps | Rest: 45s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • General: 3 sets × 10–12 reps | Rest: 75s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0

Progressive overload:
  • Beginner: +1.25kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 6 weeks
  • Intermediate: +1.25kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 4 weeks
  • Advanced: +0.5kg when top of rep range hit | Deload every 4 weeks

Muscle Group: Chest
Type: Compound
Primary Muscles: Pectoralis major (upper), Anterior deltoid

EXERCISE: Cable Fly
Muscle Group: Chest
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Pectoralis major
Secondary Muscles: Anterior deltoid
Equipment: Cables

Execution:
Stand between cable stations. Pull handles together in a wide arc, meeting at chest height. Maintain slight elbow bend throughout.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 3 sets × 5–8 reps | Rest: 120s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0
  • Hypertrophy: 3 sets × 10–15 reps | Rest: 60s | Tempo: 2-0-1-0

EXERCISE: Push-up
Muscle Group: Chest
Type: Compound
Primary Muscles: Pectoralis major
Secondary Muscles: Triceps, Anterior deltoid, Core
Equipment: Bodyweight

Execution:
Hands slightly wider than shoulder-width. Body forms straight line. Lower chest to within 2cm of floor. Push back up. Regress: knees down. Progress: feet elevated, archer push-up.

Prescription by goal:
  • Strength: 4 sets × 3–5 reps | Rest: 180s | Tempo: 2-0-X-0

EXERCISE: Dumbbell Pullover
Muscle Group: Chest
Type: Isolation
Primary Muscles: Pectoralis major, Serratus anterior
Secondary Muscles: Latissimus dorsi
Equipment: Dumbbell

Execution:
